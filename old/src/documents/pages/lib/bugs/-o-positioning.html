Resize window or change zoom to see bug

  1. All absolute or fixed positioned blocks, with both top and bottom properties specified do not recalculate their heights when we resize window only in Y axis.
  2. Even more: blocks with pixel widths do not recalculate at all.
  3. However, when we give these blocks with pixel widths percent size paddings the recalculate appears, but the lower the padding the worse the recalculating become.
